Our Founder's Story

Rooted in Tradition,
Driven by Purpose

"Ayurveda is not just medicine — it is a way of living in harmony with nature. Every product we make carries that philosophy."

Founder Name founded Divya Ayurveda in 1994 with a single mission: to bring the authentic healing wisdom of ancient India to every household — without compromise. After 12 years of research at leading Ayurvedic institutes and studying under renowned Vaidyas in Rishikesh, he established a small formulation unit that has since grown into one of India's most trusted Ayurvedic brands.

Today, Divya Ayurveda partners with Patanjali's finest herbalists, maintains GMP-certified manufacturing, and serves over 2 million families across India. The Aastha pooja range was born from his deep reverence for Vedic traditions — each product crafted to honour the sacred.
